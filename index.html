Policies & Procedures

The following Policies & Procedures form part of the agreement between the client and LivingWell Centre. They are intended to ensure a safe, respectful, and professional environment for all clients and staff. By booking or receiving services at LivingWell Centre, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to comply with these terms.

  1. Purpose of Services: All services are non-medical.
  2. Registration & Identification: Clients must register upon arrival.
  3. Alcohol/Substances: Consumption of alcohol or substances that may impair judgement is not permitted before or during treatments. We reserve the right to deny access to the centre and to services if the client is under the influence.
  4. Health Conditions: Notify the therapist of any and all health-related conditions that are relevant before receiving your massage.
  5. Cancellations: Cancel at least 24 hours in advance. Late cancellations or no shows may incur a fee.
  6. Payment: Payment is due in advance or latest at the time of service as a general rule. Accepted methods include cash and electronic payment.
  7. Safety: Notify the therapist immediately of any discomfort.
  8. Governing Law: This agreement is governed by Belgian law. Any disputes shall be resolved in the courts competent for the location of LivingWell Centre, Brussels, Belgium.

LivingWell Centre reserves the right to refuse or discontinue services in the event of non-compliance with these policies.
